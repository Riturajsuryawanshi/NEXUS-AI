# How to Use Review Intelligence

## Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Access the Feature
1. Open your Nexus Studio dashboard
2. Scroll down below the "New Analysis Session" button
3. You'll see the **Review Intelligence** section with a purple star icon

### Step 2: Get a Google Maps Business Link
1. Go to Google Maps (maps.google.com)
2. Search for any business (e.g., "Starbucks New York")
3. Click on the business to open its details
4. Copy the URL from your browser's address bar

**Example URLs that work:**
```
https://www.google.com/maps/place/Starbucks/@40.7589,-73.9851,17z/...
https://maps.google.com/?cid=12345678901234567890
https://goo.gl/maps/abc123
```

### Step 3: Analyze Reviews
1. Paste the Google Maps link into the input field at the bottom
2. Click the **"Analyze"** button (or press Enter)
3. Wait 5-10 seconds while the system processes the reviews

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Use Direct Business Links**: Copy the URL directly from the business page, not search results
2. **Popular Businesses Work Best**: Businesses with 10+ reviews provide better insights
3. **Recent Reviews**: The analysis focuses on recent review patterns
4. **Cache Duration**: Results are cached for 24 hours to save processing time

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid Google Maps link" Error
- Make sure you copied the full URL
- Try opening the business page again and copying the URL
- Ensure the URL contains "google.com/maps" or "goo.gl/maps"

### "Failed to analyze reviews" Error
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different business
- Contact support if the issue persists

### No Results Showing
- Wait a few more seconds (processing can take 5-10 seconds)
- Refresh the page and try again
- Make sure the business has reviews
